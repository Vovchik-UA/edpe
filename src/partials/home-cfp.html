EDPE 2023 Call For Papers

The 36th International Conference on Electrical Drives and Power Electronics EDPE 2023 will take place in The High Tatras, Slovakia on 25-27 September 2023. The conference is the 11th joint Slovak-Croatian conference organized by the Technical University of Košice, Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Informatics, Slovak Electrotechnical Society, University of Zagreb and KoREMA – Croatian Society for Communication, Electronics, Measurement and Control. The EDPE 2023 conference series continues in the tradition of two former conferences established independently in Slovakia and Croatia. The EDPE conference series brings opportunities for engineers, specialists in industry and academia to create a forum for sharing knowledge and exchange experiences on the recent development, applications and future trends in all aspects of power electronic systems, electrical machines, electrical drives and their industrial applications. The conference will consist of invited paper presentations, plenary sessions and poster presentations.
